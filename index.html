If you were given 1,200 dollars, what would you do with it? Would you spend it all on useless things like candy and chips, or would you spend it on groceries and utilities? This website will explain what I would do with that amount of money after each month.

This disaster caused billions of people to perish. After this happened all the countries in the world banded together to form one global nation.
